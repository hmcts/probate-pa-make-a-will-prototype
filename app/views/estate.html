Your estate

Your estate is everything you own apart from any specific gifts you decide to leave to people later on.


What it includes

  • your bank accounts and ISAs
  • property (your house or flat)
  • stocks & Shares
  • your possessions

What it doesn't include

  • most pension plans
  • most life insurance
  • specific gifts you choose to leave
  • property held as joint tenants
  • joint bank accounts

Who can you leave it with?

You can leave your estate to whoever you'd like. You can even include charities.
